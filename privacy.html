Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

ArockTV TikTok Scheduler is a private desktop/web application used by the owner of @aaroktv.

The application may store local configuration data, scheduled post data, TikTok OAuth tokens, selected video paths, captions, hashtags, and publication metadata on the owner's computer.

When the owner connects TikTok, the application uses TikTok's official OAuth flow and APIs to request account authorization and publish selected videos. Data sent to TikTok is handled according to TikTok's own terms and privacy policies.

The application does not sell personal information and does not provide public user registration. It is intended for private use by the account owner.
