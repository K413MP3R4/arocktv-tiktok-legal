ArockTV TikTok Scheduler

ArockTV TikTok Scheduler is a private desktop/web application used by the owner of @aaroktv to schedule and publish short gaming videos to their TikTok account.

The application lets the owner select videos, write captions and hashtags, schedule posts, and connect their TikTok account through TikTok's official developer platform.
